Baking Procedures

The Ingredients

  • 100 g. flour
  • 10 g. sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • salt, pepper

The Procedures

  1. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Pour in wet ingredients.
  3. Mix for 10 minutes.
  4. Bake for one hour at 300 degrees.

Notes

The recipe may be improved by adding raisins.
