Baking Procedures

The Ingredients

  • 100 g. flour
  • 10 g. sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • salt,pepper

The Procedures

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients thoroughly in a large bowl.
